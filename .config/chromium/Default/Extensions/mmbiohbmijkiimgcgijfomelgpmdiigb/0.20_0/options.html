To change the hotkeys, click on the textbox and press your desired hotkey combination. To enable/disable a hotkey, click on any button on the right.

Warning: Some shortcut combinations may not work properly. The defaults are recommended. Also, it is best to restart the browser after changing the options, since the extension will not take effect until the page is reloaded.

Note: hotkeys are disabled while on input fields.
